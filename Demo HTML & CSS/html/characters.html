The Griffin Family

Peter Griffin

Peter Griffin is the main protagonist and the patriarch of the Griffin family. He's known for his childish antics, impulsive behavior, and immature sense of humor.

Occupation: Safety Inspector at Pawtucket Brewery
Catchphrase: "Holy crap!"

Lois Griffin

Lois Griffin is Peter's wife and the matriarch of the Griffin household. She's often the voice of reason in the family, though she has her own wild side.

Occupation: Piano teacher and housewife
Maiden name: Lois Pewterschmidt

Stewie Griffin

Stewie Griffin is the youngest child of Peter and Lois. Despite being a baby, he speaks with an advanced vocabulary and British accent. Early in the series, he was bent on world domination and killing Lois, but his character evolved over time.

Notable traits: Genius intellect, time machine inventor
Best friend: Brian Griffin

Brian Griffin

Brian is the Griffin family's anthropomorphic pet dog. He walks upright, talks, has human-level intelligence, and forms human-like relationships with people.

Occupation: Writer, occasional jobs
Interests: Literature, politics, martinis

Meg Griffin

Meg is the oldest Griffin child and is frequently the butt of jokes and abuse from her family, especially Peter. She's often depicted as unpopular and insecure.

Age: Teenager (high school student)
Friends: Very few, occasionally makes and loses friends

Chris Griffin

Chris is the middle child of Peter and Lois. He's portrayed as being overweight, unintelligent, and socially awkward, but good-hearted.

Age: Teenager
Special talent: Drawing
