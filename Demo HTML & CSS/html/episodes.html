Notable Episodes

Blue Harvest

One of the most famous Family Guy episodes is "Blue Harvest," which parodies Star Wars Episode IV: A New Hope with Family Guy characters playing the Star Wars roles.

Season: 6, Episode: 1
Original air date: September 23, 2007

This episode began a trilogy of Star Wars parodies, followed by "Something, Something, Something, Dark Side" and "It's A Trap!"

Road to... Episodes

The "Road to..." episodes feature Brian and Stewie in various adventures inspired by the classic "Road to..." films starring Bob Hope and Bing Crosby.

These episodes include:

  • Road to Rhode Island (Season 2)
  • Road to Europe (Season 3)
  • Road to Rupert (Season 5)
  • Road to Germany (Season 7)
  • Road to the Multiverse (Season 8)
  • And several more...

Life of Brian

In this controversial episode, Brian Griffin is killed after being hit by a car. The Griffin family mourns his death and gets a new dog named Vinny.

Season: 12, Episode: 6
Original air date: November 24, 2013

However, two episodes later in "Christmas Guy," Stewie uses his time machine to save Brian's life, bringing him back to the show.
